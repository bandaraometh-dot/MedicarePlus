Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment online through our booking system, call us directly, or visit our clinic during business hours.

What insurance do you accept?

We accept most major insurance providers. Please contact our billing department for specific coverage details.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we have emergency services available 24/7. For emergencies, please call our emergency line immediately.

Can I get my test results online?

Yes, test results are available through our patient portal. You'll need to create an account to access your medical records.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your insurance card, photo ID, list of current medications, and any relevant medical records.

Do you have parking facilities?

Yes, we offer free parking for all patients and visitors in our dedicated parking lot.
